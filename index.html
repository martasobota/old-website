Welcome to my website!

Hi! I'm Marta Zaryn. I went to the University of Economics and the University of Life Science. For three years I was a landscape architect. During preparation of one of my analysis, I got to know programming for the first time. I liked it so much, that I decided to rebrand.

Now I'm a student at Back-End Bootcamp at CodersLab (Python + Django + JavaScript). My passion are green roofs (those with plants, not only paint). I prepare maps and analysis for existing and potential green roofs in urban areas. I was a participant of Oddysey of the Mind programme for 5 years, so solving problems is a kind of my hobby.

I find it inspiring to encourage newbies to programming, so if you have a question or just want to say hi, send me an e-mail.
